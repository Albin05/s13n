## Post-class Quiz: Creating and Initializing Sets

### Q1: What creates an empty set?
**Answer: B) `set()`**

### Q2: What does `{1, 2, 2, 3}` output?
**Answer: A) `{1, 2, 3}`**

### Q3: What can't be in a set?
**Answer: C) Lists**

### Q4: `len(set([1, 2, 2, 3]))`?
**Answer: B) 3**

### Q5: Sets are ordered?
**Answer: B) False**
