### **27. Process Menu with Validation**

Create a calculator menu. Skip invalid choices without counting them.

**Example:**
```
Calculator:
1. Add
2. Subtract
3. Multiply
4. Divide
5. Quit

Choice: 9
Invalid choice!
Choice: 1
Enter numbers: 10 5
Result: 15
Choice: 5
Goodbye!
```

**Hint:** while True, break on "5", continue on invalid choice.
