### **10. Sum Positive Numbers**

Calculate sum and average of positive numbers only from a list.

Given list: `[10, -5, 20, -3, 15, -8, 5]`

**Expected Output:**
```
Positive sum: 50
Positive count: 4
Average: 12.5
```

**Hint:** Skip negatives with continue, accumulate sum and count for positives.
