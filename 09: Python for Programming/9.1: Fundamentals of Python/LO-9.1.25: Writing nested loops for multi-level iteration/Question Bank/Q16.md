### **16. Prime Number Grid**

Check which positions in a 2D list contain prime numbers.

Given matrix: `[[2, 4, 5], [6, 7, 9], [10, 11, 13]]`

**Expected Output:**
```
Prime at (0, 0): 2
Prime at (0, 2): 5
Prime at (1, 1): 7
Prime at (2, 1): 11
Prime at (2, 2): 13
```

**Hint:** Nested loops with is_prime helper function.
