## Problem 6: Multiples of 5 (Medium)

### Solution
```python
for num in range(5, 51, 5):
    print(num)
```

**Output:**
```
5
10
15
20
25
30
35
40
45
50
```

### Explanation
- Start at 5, stop before 51, step by 5
- Generates multiples of 5
- 10 numbers total
- Step parameter creates the pattern
- Alternative: `range(1, 11)` with `i * 5`
