### **29. Nested Multiplication**

Create a simple multiplication grid (1-5) using nested range loops.

**Expected Output:**
```
1  2  3  4  5
2  4  6  8  10
3  6  9  12 15
4  8  12 16 20
5  10 15 20 25
```

**Hint:** Outer `range(1, 6)` for i, inner `range(1, 6)` for j, print `i*j` with proper spacing.
