### **12. Multiplication Table**

Generate a multiplication table for the number 7 (from 7×1 to 7×10) using range().

**Expected Output:**
```
7 × 1 = 7
7 × 2 = 14
7 × 3 = 21
7 × 4 = 28
7 × 5 = 35
7 × 6 = 42
7 × 7 = 49
7 × 8 = 56
7 × 9 = 63
7 × 10 = 70
```

**Hint:** Use `range(1, 11)` and multiply 7 by each number i.
