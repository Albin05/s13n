### **17. Bank Account Simulator**

Simulate a bank account with initial balance $1000. Options:
1. Deposit
2. Withdraw
3. Quit

Validate transactions (positive amounts, sufficient balance).

**Example Interaction:**
```
Balance: $1000
1. Deposit
2. Withdraw
3. Quit
Choose action: 1
Deposit amount: $500
Deposited $500

Balance: $1500
1. Deposit
2. Withdraw
3. Quit
Choose action: 2
Withdraw amount: $200
Withdrew $200

Balance: $1300
1. Deposit
2. Withdraw
3. Quit
Choose action: 3
Thank you!
Final balance: $1300
```

**Hint:** Loop until action == "quit", validate amounts before updating balance.
