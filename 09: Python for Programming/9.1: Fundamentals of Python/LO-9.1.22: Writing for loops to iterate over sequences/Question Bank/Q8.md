### **8. Combine Two Lists**

Print names and ages together from two separate lists.

Given:
- Names: `["Alice", "Bob", "Charlie"]`
- Ages: `[25, 30, 35]`

**Expected Output:**
```
Alice is 25 years old
Bob is 30 years old
Charlie is 35 years old
```

**Hint:** Use `zip(names, ages)` to pair them together.
