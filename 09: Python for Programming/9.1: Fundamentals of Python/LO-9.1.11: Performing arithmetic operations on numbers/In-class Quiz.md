# Post-Class Quiz: Perform Arithmetic Operations

## Q1: What does 10 / 2 return?
A) 5
B) 5.0
C) Error

<details><summary>Answer</summary>
B - 5.0 (division always returns float)
</details>

## Q2: What's 17 // 5?
A) 3
B) 3.4
C) 2

<details><summary>Answer</summary>
A - 3 (integer division drops decimal)
</details>

## Q3: What's 17 % 5?
A) 2
B) 3
C) 3.4

<details><summary>Answer</summary>
A - 2 (remainder: 17 = 5×3 + 2)
</details>

## Q4: What's 2 ** 4?
A) 8
B) 16
C) 6

<details><summary>Answer</summary>
B - 16 (2⁴ = 2×2×2×2)
</details>

## Q5: After this code, what's x?
```python
x = 10
x += 5
x *= 2
```
A) 20
B) 25
C) 30

<details><summary>Answer</summary>
C - 30 (10 + 5 = 15, then 15 × 2 = 30)
</details>
