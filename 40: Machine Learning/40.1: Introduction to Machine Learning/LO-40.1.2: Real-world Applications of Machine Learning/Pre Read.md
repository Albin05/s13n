## Real-World Example: Email Spam Filters
One of the earliest and most successful applications of ML.
* **Old Way:** A human writes rules: "Block emails with the word 'Winner'."
* **ML Way:** The system looks at millions of emails marked as "Spam" by users and learns that the combination of "Winner," "Click here," and "Urgent" usually means spam, even if the sender changes the spelling to "W1nner."
